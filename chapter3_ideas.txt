A shadowy sorcerer who thrives on forgotten memories, growing stronger as names fade and pasts slip into oblivion’s grasp.
Elara uncovers a scorched parchment beneath twisted roots, bearing half an ancient rune pulsing faintly—like it’s waiting to be completed.
Elara meets Squeaky, a hyperactive, time-traveling squirrel who speaks in riddles and insists he once dated a pocket watch.
