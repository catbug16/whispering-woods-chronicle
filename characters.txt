Elara, a spirited sixteen-year-old with wild chestnut curls and eyes the color of stormy skies, had always yearned for more than village tales. Brave and endlessly curious, she explored beyond the familiar paths. When she uncovered a weathered map beneath a fallen oak, her heart raced—adventure had finally found her.

Shadow, a sleek wolf with midnight-black fur and silver eyes, had followed Elara since she saved him as a pup. Fiercely loyal and eerily perceptive, he moved like a whisper through the underbrush. Where Elara ventured, Shadow was never far behind—her silent guardian in a forest full of secrets.
