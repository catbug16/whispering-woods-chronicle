**1. The Language of Ancient Runes**
The Language of Ancient Runes is a forgotten script woven with temporal magic. Each symbol carries echoes of time—past, present, and possible futures. Only Chronomancers can safely interpret them, as misreading a rune may trigger memory loops or collapse moments into one. Runes often glow when truth is near.

**2. The Griffin of the Sky Peaks**
High above the clouds, the Griffin guards the Sky Peaks—majestic, golden-feathered, and bound by an ancient oath. With the wings of an eagle and the heart of a lion, it watches time’s flow from its perch. Legends say it speaks only to those who’ve glimpsed both future sorrow and past hope.
